Strategy for the Development of Bolivia’s Tourism Offer

Project description

Indigenous tourism experiences were designed in communities in the Uyuni Salt Flat and the Bolivian Amazon through a participatory process that integrated workshops, collective co-creation and field surveys to structure proposals based on local potential. Results included a strategy for the sustainable development of the national tourism offer and institutional strengthening; a Methodological Guide for the Design of Innovative Tourism Experiences; a catalogue of experiences in pilot areas; and an investment attraction program. Together, these outputs contribute to diversifying the offer, improving destination competitiveness and energizing local economies.
