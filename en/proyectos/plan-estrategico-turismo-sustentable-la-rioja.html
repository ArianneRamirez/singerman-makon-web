Strategic Sustainable Tourism Plan for La Rioja Province

Project description

The PLANTUR La Rioja 360 was formulated through a broad participatory process that brought together more than 600 people in regional workshops and included more than 70 interviews with public and private stakeholders. The result is a strategic plan to promote sustainable tourism development in a territory of 89,680 km² and 384,000 inhabitants, with a five-year horizon, structured into four action programs and 67 projects aimed at strengthening competitiveness, territorial coordination and the generation of economic opportunities at the provincial level.
