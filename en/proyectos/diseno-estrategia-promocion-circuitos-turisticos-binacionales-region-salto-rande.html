Design and Promotion Strategy for Binational Tourism Circuits in the Salto Grande Region

Project description

Thematic tourism circuits were co-created and a Promotion and Positioning Plan was designed for their commercialization, integrating services and attractions from the regional value chain. These products were developed through a participatory process involving more than 300 stakeholders from the tourism system in the Salto Grande Region, a binational territory of 24,000 km² between Argentina and Uruguay, with an estimated population of 466,000 inhabitants.
