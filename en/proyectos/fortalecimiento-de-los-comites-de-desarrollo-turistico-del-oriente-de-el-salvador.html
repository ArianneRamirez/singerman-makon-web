Strengthening Tourism Development Committees in Eastern El Salvador

Project description

Management capacities were strengthened for 160 members of 13 Tourism Development Committees in four Specialized Tourism Destinations in the eastern region of the country. The work included the design and implementation of a training program based on a “learning by doing” approach, participatory formulation of work plans and inventories of the tourism offer. These inputs were later integrated into an Interactive Map WebApp developed as part of the consultancy, serving as a destination management and promotion tool.
