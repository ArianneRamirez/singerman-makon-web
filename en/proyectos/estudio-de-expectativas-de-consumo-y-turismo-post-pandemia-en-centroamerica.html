Post-Pandemic Consumer and Tourism Expectations Study in Central America

Project description

A market research study was developed to identify tourism consumption expectations, travel decisions and emerging trends in Central American countries in the post-COVID-19 pandemic context. The study generated strategic information for the design of action plans, helping to mitigate the effects of the crisis and accelerate the recovery of tourism and economic activity in the region.
