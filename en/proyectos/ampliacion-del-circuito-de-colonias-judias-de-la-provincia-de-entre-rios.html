Expansion of the Jewish Colonies Circuit in Entre Ríos Province

Project description

The “Rincones con Historia” Plan was updated and implemented, expanding the original scope—focused on the provincial central corridor—to include towns along the Uruguay River corridor and northern Entre Ríos. This consolidated a comprehensive strategy for an area of 32,730 km² and a population of nearly 460,000 inhabitants. Key results were generated for tourism positioning and activation, including the participatory design of the brand “Colonias Judías de Entre Ríos. Una estrella, miles de historias”, the development of a Catalogue of Tourism Experiences and the conceptual design of “La Gran Fiesta de la Colectividad Judía Entrerriana”. The process involved more than 150 community leaders and stakeholders, strengthening territorial ownership, public-community coordination and the enhancement of cultural heritage as a driver of local development.
